FBLA: Electric Boogaloo

This app is a mixture of a proof of concept for an Electron app as well as a revamp for my senior project. During my senior year of high school I was enterd into the FBLA Desktop Application competion. The task for that year was to create a desktop application that would store information of all the member of FBLA. The intial revision of that application was written with VB.NET and used an Access file as the "Database". It was functional and did everything that the specs required but there was no SQL in the entire source code. Hearing that Node js and Electron offered cross platform app development and deployment, I knew exactly what to use for my first crack at that framework.
